<p><p>CBRNe protective clothing is categorized into two main types: gas-tight and gas-permeable. Gas-tight clothing is designed to provide a complete seal against chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear threats, ensuring no contaminants can penetrate the fabric. It is typically used in high-risk scenarios. In contrast, gas-permeable clothing allows for breathability while offering some level of protection. This type is suitable for lower-risk environments, where comfort and mobility are prioritized alongside partial protection from hazardous materials.</p></p>
