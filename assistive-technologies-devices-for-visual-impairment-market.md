<p><p>The assistive technologies market for visual impairment includes several key devices. Braille displays convert text to Braille, allowing users to read digital content tactilely. Note takers facilitate text input and organization, often equipped with Braille keyboards. Magnifiers enhance visual accessibility by enlarging text or images. Braille printers and embossers produce embossed Braille documents for physical reading. Lastly, Braille writers enable users to create Braille text manually, supporting independent writing and communication for individuals with visual impairments.</p></p>
