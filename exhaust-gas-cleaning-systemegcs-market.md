<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System(EGCS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust Gas Cleaning Systems (EGCS) are used to reduce sulfur emissions from ships and industrial processes. There are three main types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop EGCS** utilizes seawater to wash exhaust gases, discharging treated water back into the sea.</p><p>2. **Closed Loop EGCS** recycles water within the system, treating it before discharge, making it suitable for areas with strict regulations.</p><p>3. **Mixed Waste Gas Purification Systems** combine both open and closed-loop techniques, allowing flexibility based on operational conditions and regulatory demands.</p></p>
