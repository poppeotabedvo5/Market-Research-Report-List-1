<p><p>The Tritium Batteries market is divided into two primary types: Thermal Conversion Batteries and Non-Thermal Conversion Batteries. Thermal Conversion Batteries utilize heat to release energy from Tritium, enabling efficient power generation, particularly in specialized applications. Non-Thermal Conversion Batteries, on the other hand, harness Tritium's radioactivity directly without the need for heat, providing a more compact and long-lasting energy source. Both types are crucial for applications in aerospace, military, and remote power systems where reliability and longevity are essential.</p></p>
