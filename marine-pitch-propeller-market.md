<p><strong>The Marine Pitch Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine pitch propeller market comprises two main types: fixed pitch propellers and controllable pitch propellers. Fixed pitch propellers have blades set at a specific angle, providing consistent performance but limited flexibility. They are typically used in vessels with steady operational profiles. In contrast, controllable pitch propellers allow for dynamic adjustment of blade angle, enhancing maneuverability and fuel efficiency in varying conditions. This adaptability makes them suitable for vessels requiring precise control, such as tugboats and cargo ships.</p></p>
