<p><p>The inorganic nanoparticles market is categorized primarily into two types: powder and dispersion liquid. Powder-based nanoparticles are solid-state materials used in various applications such as catalysts, pigments, and electronics due to their high stability and concentration. In contrast, dispersion liquids consist of nanoparticles suspended in a liquid medium, facilitating easier application in fields like pharmaceuticals, coatings, and inks. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, influenced by factors like reactivity, ease of processing, and end-use performance.</p></p>
