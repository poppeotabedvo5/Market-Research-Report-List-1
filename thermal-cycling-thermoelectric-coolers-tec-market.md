<p><p>Thermal Cycling Thermoelectric Coolers (TECs) are devices that use the Peltier effect to create a temperature differential for cooling applications. The market is segmented into Single Stage and Multistage Thermal Cycling TECs. Single Stage TECs are designed for moderate cooling needs, offering simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, Multistage TECs utilize multiple thermoelectric junctions to achieve lower temperatures, making them suitable for advanced applications requiring significant cooling power. Both types are critical in electronics, medical devices, and telecommunications.</p></p>
