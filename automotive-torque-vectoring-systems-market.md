<p><ul><li>Active Torque Vectoring System (ATVS)</li><li>Passive Torque Vectoring System (PTVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Torque Vectoring Systems market is categorized into two main types: Active Torque Vectoring Systems (ATVS) and Passive Torque Vectoring Systems (PTVS). ATVS utilizes electronic controls to dynamically distribute torque between wheels based on driving conditions, enhancing traction and stability. In contrast, PTVS relies on mechanical systems to provide a fixed amount of torque distribution, improving handling and cornering without the need for complex electronics. Both systems aim to optimize vehicle performance and safety by improving traction and control.</p></p>
